üîÑ Segment 2: Best-Effort vs. Deterministic Networking (7 minutes)

Content to Deliver:

Best-Effort Networking: Variable latency, no guarantees, packet loss possible

Deterministic Networking: Guaranteed delivery, predictable latency, time-synchronized

Introduce Time-Sensitive Networking (TSN) as the solution framework

Comparison Table:

Characteristic Best-Effort (Wi-Fi) Deterministic (TSN)
Latency Variable (50-200ms) Guaranteed (<10ms)
Packet Loss Possible Zero tolerance (FRER)
Interference High susceptibility Isolated, managed
Scalability Limited (50-100 devices) Massive (1000s of devices)

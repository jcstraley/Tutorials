üìñ About Nepali Pronunciation

For American speakers: Nepali uses Devanagari script. Vowels are similar to Spanish: A (ah), E (eh), I (ee), O (oh), U (oo). The aspirated consonants (like "kh", "th", "ph") are pronounced with a puff of air. "Cha" sounds like "cha" in "church".

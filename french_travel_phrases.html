üéØ Cultural & Pronunciation Tips

Always greet first: Say "Bonjour" before asking questions or making requests
Formal vs Informal: Use "vous" (formal) with strangers, shopkeepers, and older people
Silent letters: Final consonants are usually silent (Paris = pah-REE, not pah-REES)
Nasal sounds (~): Let air flow through nose and mouth simultaneously
R sound: Guttural, from the back of throat (not rolled like Spanish)
